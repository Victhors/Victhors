## About Me

I'm an **AI & MLOps Engineer focused on LLM Security** — I build LLM systems and the secure, observable infrastructure that keeps them in production: deployable, private, and hard to break.

At **Youlex** (legaltech), I contributed to a **hybrid-RAG legal search engine** (BM25 + dense vector + RRF + BGE reranker over TCU case law with Legal-BERTimbau), reaching **Recall@5 of 94%** — plus the MLOps around it: an **8-stage pipeline with zero-PII sanitization** and a **PII-safe staging environment** with an automated safety gate. As an independent consultant, I shipped **WhatsApp AI agents** to production (n8n, VPS, Docker, Postgres/Supabase, Redis) with human handoff and observability, **cutting response time from 6–12h to 1–3min** at **24/7** uptime. At **Outlier**, I work freelance on **LLM evaluation** — designing agentic eval tasks and scoring model trajectories, including prompt-injection testing.

I also lead **Ares**, a cybersecurity / DevSecOps extension project at UFC. Currently preparing for the **CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator)**.
